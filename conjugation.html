a. Prepare 2 mL Zeba column to equilibriate unconjugated antibody in Bicarbonate buffer

  1. Break off stopper and spin once at 1000 x g for 2 minutes to remove storage solution.
  2. Add 1 mL 0.1 M Bicarbonate buffer, spin at 1000 x g for 2 minutes. Repeat this step once more.
  3. Add 1 mL 0.1 M Bicarbonate buffer, spin at 1000 x g for 3 minutes.
  4. Add 1 mL of your unconjugated antibody, spin at 1000 x g for 3 minutes and transfer the solution to an amber tube.

b. Prepare Alexa fluors for conjugation

  1. Bring desiccated fluors to room temperature.
  2. Resuspend each fluor (1 mg) with 100 uL of fresh DMSO to make a 10mg/ml solution and vortex to ensure proper mixing.
  3. Add the indicated amount of fluor solution (use conjugation calculator) to the tube and flick to mix.
  4. Incubate for 2 hours while rotating at room temperature.

c. Prepare 2 mL Zeba column to transfer conjunated antibody to its formulation buffer

  1. Break off stopper and spin once (1000 x g for 2 minutes) to remove storage solution.
  2. Add 1 mL formulation buffer, spin at 1000 x g for 2 minutes, aspirate flow through. Repeat this step once more.
  3. Add 1 mL formulation buffer, spin at 1000 x g for 3 minutes, aspirate flow through.
  4. Get a new 15 mL conical for collection.
  5. Add all conjugated antibody-fluor mix to the column, spin at 1000 x g for 3 minutes and collect flowthrough (conjugated antibody) in the 15 ml conical tube.
  6. Store the conjugated antibody in amber tubes at 4C.

Reagents

  • ZebaT Spin Desalting Columns, 40K MWCO, 2 mL
  • Alexa FluorsT NHS (Succinimidyl Esters)
  • DMSO
